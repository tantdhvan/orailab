We are

ORLab

ORLab, "Phòng thí nghiệm tối ưu hóa các hệ thống lớn" in Vietnamese name is one of the first research laboratories on Operations Research in Vietnam. We provide consulting services, software, and solutions for optimizing operations in companies, societies, and public sectors, etc to reduce cost and increase efficiency. Our vision is to become a leading research center on Operations Research in Vietnam with high reputation at international level.
